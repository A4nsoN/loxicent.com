通过打孔金属片和压电陶瓷的巧妙结合，微孔雾化片能将液体轻松打成微细颗粒，因其低噪音以及低成本的特点，在香氛扩散和加湿补水等多种产品中得到了广泛的应用。 微孔雾化片的正常工作，需要经由适当的驱动和控制电路配合，提供必要的电能和振动激励，以及合适的工作频率等参数。
LOXIM最新微孔雾化驱动控制芯片，以独家专利硬件和软件技术，更近一步集成到专用芯片，解决了微孔雾化行业一直以来的雾化一致性与功率焦虑问题；智能化的控制逻辑，能更灵活敏捷地适配微孔雾化片，进一步降低整体成本。
With precisely engineered structure of perforated stainless-steel mesh and piezoelectric element, mesh-vibrating diffuser transforms the liquid into a fine mist. Thanks to this low-cost technology, it was widely used for desk humidifiers and scenting devices.

While this mesh-vibrating is a mature technology, it requires appropriate system for energy and signal control, as well as right parameter for resonating.

The latest solution from LOXIM, patented hardware and software design, provides a new and better solution to this mature industry. It addresses the primary concerns of engineers on the consistency of misting performance and agility to match broader scope of misting units. With the integration of whole design into a chip, it also brings significant cost saving to the total BOM cost.
