无水雾化模组是LOXIM团队自主研发设计的、由泵送系统和针式振动雾化系统经过模块化封装成的专利产品。泵送系统将液体经由管道输送至针式振动雾化系统处，并在雾化系统处通过振动雾化的方式将液体打碎成微小的细雾颗粒。模组的内部整体结构是封闭设计，液体是不会直接接触到泵体和振动体的。 模组尺寸精巧易与部署、噪音及功耗低，且广泛适配不同粘度的精油，可实现稳定的定量泵送与雾化。
The Waterless-Diffusing Engine is a patented technology developed and designed by LOXIM team. The Engine integrates a pumping system and a vibrating system. The pumping system delivers liquid to the vibrating system via a tube while the vibrating system breaks the liquid into tiny fine mist particles and diffuses out. The whole internal structure of this Engine is a closed design, so that the liquid could not directly contact with the pump unit and the vibrating unit.

The Engine is with compact size and flexible for ID design, operating in low noise and low power. Besides, it is compatible with most kinds of essential oils regardless viscosities——there is no need to worry about clogging. Stable performance of accurate displacement in pumping and diffusing can be achieved as well
